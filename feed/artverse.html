What is a Plantoid?

Plantoids are blockchain-based life forms that live on the Ethereum network. They are autonomous, self-sufficient, and capable of reproducing themselves.

Like plants, Plantoids rely on external contributions to reproduce. When you feed a Plantoid with cryptocurrency, you receive a unique digital seed (NFT) created through generative AI.
